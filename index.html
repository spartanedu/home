FOREGOING PROBLEMS

Challenges, Higher Educational Institutions report, and Implications

Challenges

In all places, Education is the key to success. There are challenges facing all students globally as they progress through their academic persuit. These inlude getting adequate information and orientation on the opportunities opened by the Higher Education and Job Market. They also include choosing the right subjects, courses, professions and higher institutions.

Higher Educational Institutions Report

Higher Educational Institutions have complained that most of the students disqualify themselves long before applying. The reason is, they do not know much about their structure, programmes, and requirements. By the time they realized it, it becomes too late to make amendments.

Implications

The current problems have serious implications for the individuals, their families, and the national development.
